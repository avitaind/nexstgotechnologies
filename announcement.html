Nexstgo Technologies India Pvt Ltd announces Letter of Intent to collaborate with Panache

19th February 2022: Nexstgo Technologies India Pvt Ltd is pleased to announce that it has signed a Letter of Intent with Panache Digilife Limited, a contract development and manufacturing organization (CDMO) catering to Information, Communication, and Technology (ICT) Devices providing solutions from Design, Manufacture, and Service in the Lifetime management of IT Devices. Nexstgo is now in contract with Panache for the manufacture and supply of 200,000 laptop devices under its brand name AVITA for the period of the next one year.

Ms. Seema Bhatnagar, Regional Business Director (South Asia, and MEA) Nexstgo Company Limited said, “Nexstgo is elated to join hands with Panache, This partnership falls in line with our focus on Make in India . That being said, we are confident of our plans to achieve a double digit market share this year .”

Speaking of the collaboration, Nikit Rambhia, Joint Managing Director of Panache Digilife Limited, said, “This order will provide an impetus for Panache’s plans in complying with its commitment to the Make in India initiatives promoted by the Government of India and incentivized under the Production Linked Incentive Scheme for IT Hardware.”

Nexstgo Technologies India Pvt Ltd is committed to bringing new revolutionary experiences in technology through introducing a wide product range, which includes PC, Smart home, IoT, artificial intelligence, and corporate solutions under the brand name AVITA. AVITA believes in the wonder and power of the individual. It makes premium technology products that are suitable for professionals and millennials alike.

What makes AVITA standout in the tech market is its range of premium, budget-friendly, and diligently crafted laptops for today’s youth and professionals. AVITA offers an array of laptops including the Reddot Winner 2021 Liber V14, ESSENTIAL, COSMOS 2-in-1, and PURA E. AVITA will also be entering the smart lighting world and other IoT devices under the category name DOMUS.

AVITA has successfully set a niche for itself in countries like India and Bangladesh. It aims to grow bigger in the South Asian tech market by launching itself in MEA and Nepal. Now, it looks forward to becoming a tech-leading brand and serving customers with the best of services.
